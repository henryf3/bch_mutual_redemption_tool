Mutual Redemption Proposal Generation

If you are using in this contract a pair different than USD/BCH, read the instructions below.

1. Get your asset value compared to 1 BCH. For XAU: 1 BCH = 0,1382 XAU

2. Get the price in USD dollars. In this case: 0,1382 XAU = 336.31 USD

3. Used the obtained value in step 2 to generate the proposal (336.31)

For conversions you can use: www.xe.com
